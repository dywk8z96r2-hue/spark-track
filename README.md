this progressive web app (PWA) is for walmart spark drivers to better keep track of their trips as the spark app does not currently have a feature to export trip data
you track your trips in this app along your trip in the spark app and you can add additional data such as odometer readings to track miles,
your vehicles MPG, and current gas price,
to better track your miles (for tax purposes) and how much you spend on gas
you can export your data as a csv (spreadsheet) or json (metadata) file
exported data shows all your inputted data as calculates miles driven, time spent on each segment of your trip,
estimated amount of gas used and estimated cost of gas used based on the current price of gas you can input in the settings tab
to use this PWA go to https://dywk8z96r2-hue.github.io/spark-track/ in safari go to share>add to home screen
be sure the "open as web app" toggle is on
this is currently optimized for iphones but its all html so theres probably a way to use it on android.
